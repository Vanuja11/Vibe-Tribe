American singer and rapper Lizzo's fourth studio album Special's lead single, "About Damn Time," was made available on April 14, 2022, through Nice Life Recordings and Atlantic Records (2022). The song peaked at number one in the US, becoming her second number-one single there, and also peaked in the top three in ten other nations. It also peaked at number six on the Billboard Global 200 chart.

Harry Styles' third studio album, Harry's Home, was released on May 20, 2022, by Columbia Records and Erskine. Harry Styles is an English singer and songwriter. The album, which has been hailed as Styles' most contemplative work, was mostly written and recorded between 2020 and 2021. The pop-funk, pop rock, and synth-pop album Harry's House was influenced by the Japanese city pop of the 1970s.

The title track of American singer and multi-instrumentalist Bonnie Raitt's 18th studio album, Just Like That, which was released on April 22, 2022 by Redwing Records, is the song "Just Like That." The song's lyrics tell the tale of a mom who receives a visit from the person who received her son's heart during a life-saving organ donation procedure. It was written and produced by Raitt.

Adele, an English singer, has a song called "Easy on Me" in her fourth studio album 30. (2021). Along with Greg Kurstin, the song's producer, Adele wrote it. On October 15, 2021, Columbia Records released it as the album's lead single. It is a sparsely produced pop ballad that is accompanied by a prominent, somber piano and driven by light bass beats. The lyrics are Adele's request to her son, in which she describes her troubles with her disintegrated marriage and asks him to be kind to her. The lyrics express themes of nostalgia, regret, and forgiveness.

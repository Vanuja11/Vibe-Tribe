History

Rock music is a genre of popular music that originated in the United States in the 1950s. It has its roots in a variety of musical genres, including rhythm and blues, country music, and gospel music. The first rock and roll records were made by African American musicians such as Chuck Berry, Little Richard, and Fats Domino. Rock music quickly spread to other parts of the world, including the United Kingdom, where bands like The Beatles, The Rolling Stones, and The Who became popular in the 1960s.

Characteristics of Rock Music
  • Loud electric guitars
  • Strong drum beats
  • Powerful vocals
  • Fast-paced rhythms
  • Emphasis on live performances
Categorizing Rock Music

There is no certain answer to "what is Rock Music?". It is actually a spectrum. And we can break down Rock Music furthermore. The classification is as followed;

  • Garage rock
  • Blues rock
  • Folk rock
  • Progressive rock
  • Jazz rock

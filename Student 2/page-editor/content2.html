Indie Music

Indie music is like the rebel of the music world. It's produced and distributed independently, without the help of big record labels. This DIY approach gives indie musicians a chance to be creative and experimental, creating a unique sound that sets them apart from the mainstream. Indie music covers a variety of genres, from rock to electronic to hip-hop.

TOP INDIE MUSIC ARTISTS

Indie music has always been a breeding ground for fresh talent and dope sounds. From the gritty 90s bedroom recordings to the sick beats of today's indie scene, there's always something new to discover in the world of independent music. But who are the must-know indie artists? Even though they stay true to their DIY roots and don't bow down to the mainstream, a few indie acts have still managed to break through and snag major success.


TOP 10 INDIE ARTISTS OF 2022


1. MITSKI


Mitski Miyawaki is an American singer-songwriter of Japanese descent. While studying studio composition at Purchase College's Conservatory of Music, Mitski self-released her first two albums, Lush (2012) and Retired from Sad, New Profession in Business (2013). According to The Guardian, she is the "best young songwriter" in the United States.



2. WET LEG


Wet Leg, a British indie rock duo from the Isle of Wight, was formed in 2019 by Rhian Teasdale and Hester Chambers. Their debut single "Chaise Longue" quickly topped three million streams in 2021.



3. CONAN GREY


Conan Lee Gray is a social media personality and recording artist from the United States. He is best known for his debut EP, "Sunset Season," which was published by Republic Records in 2018 and has received over 160 million streams online. Gray has sold out two North American tours and one European tour since 2018, as well as supporting Panic! at the Disco on their Pray for the Wicked Tour, and has amassed over 250 million streams.



4. MAGGIE ROGERS


Margaret Debay Rogers is an Easton, Maryland-born American singer-songwriter and record producer. Her major break occurred when Pharrell Williams heard her song "Alaska" during a master class at New York University's Clive Davis Center of Recorded Music. In 2019, she was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best New Artist.



5. ANIMAL COLLECTIVE


Animal Collective is an American experimental pop band founded in 2003 in Baltimore, Maryland. Avey Tare (David Portner), Panda Bear (Noah Lennox), Geologist (Brian Weitz), and Deakin are among its members (Josh Dibb). The band's work is distinguished by an eclectic investigation of forms such as psychedelia, freak folk, noise, and electronica, with the usage of elements such as loops, drones, vocal harmonies, and sound collage.



6. KURT VILE


Kurt Samuel Vile is a singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and record producer from the United States. He is well-known for his solo work as well as his time as the lead guitarist for the rock band The War on Drugs. Vile is joined by his backing band, The Violators, both in the studio and during live concerts. Inspired by Pavement, Neil Young, Tom Petty, and John Fahey, Vile began his musical career with lo-fi home recordings and several solo projects.



7. LAUV


Ari Staprans Leff, professionally known as Lauv, is an American singer and musician best known for his breakout single "I Like Me Better," which appears on his 2018 compilation album I Met You When I Was 18 (The Playlist). How I'm Feeling, his debut album, was released on March 6, 2020, and debuted at number 16 on the US Billboard 200.



8. MUNA


Katie Gavin, Josette Maskin, and Naomi McPherson make up the American indie pop band MUNA. They signed with Saddest Factory Records and released two studio albums with RCA Records, About U (2017) and Saves the World (2019), before signing with Saddest Factory Records, which released their third studio album, Muna, in June 2022.



9. KING PRINCESS


Mikaela Mullaney Straus, better known as King Princess, is a Brooklyn-based American pop singer, composer, and multi-instrumentalist. She is contracted to Mark Ronson's label Zelig Records, which is a Columbia Records imprint. King Princess released her debut single "1950" from the Make My Bed EP in February 2018. The song was a commercial success, charting in many areas and eventually earning platinum certification from the Recording Industry Association of America.



10. SPOON


Spoon is an Austin, Texas-based American rock band. Britt Daniel, Jim Eno, Rob Pope, Eric Harvey, and Alex Fischel make up the band.
